About the Studio

Crafting Spaces
with Intention

Founded in 2008, Shree Design Studio is an award-winning architecture and interior design practice based in Ahmedabad. We believe great architecture is born at the intersection of cultural sensitivity, spatial intelligence, and enduring craft. Every project we undertake is a deeply personal collaboration with our clients — a journey from vision to lived reality.

◈
Precision
Meticulous attention to every detail, material, and proportion.
◇

◫

Responsible design that honours the earth and future generations.
